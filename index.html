About Bexley Linkup

Bexley Linkup (Blink) was created by a group of small business owners with a mission to connect sole traders, start-ups, entrepreneurs and established businesses in the London Borough of Bexley and the surrounding areas through relaxed and informal networking events.

The group meets on the second Wednesday of every month at a convenient location in Bexley. Our events are open to all, and are free to attend.

Come and join us at our next meetup to experience the freedom of natural networking.
